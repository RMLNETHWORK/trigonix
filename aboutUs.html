ABOUT US

TrigoniX is a free, student-built web application designed to simplify trigonometry for everyone.
Whether you're solving for angles, sides, or identities, TrigoniX offers clean, intuitive tools that work seamlessly across desktop and mobile.
Created by young developers passionate about education and accessibility, the platform is tailored for students, teachers, and anyone needing fast, reliable trigonometric calculations.
With a focus on usability, responsiveness, and future expansion, TrigoniX is your pocket-sized companion for learning and mastering trigonometry.
