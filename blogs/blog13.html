🎮 Trigonometry in Computer Graphics

Every frame of modern 3D animation involves thousands of trigonometric calculations for lighting, perspective, and motion.

Core Techniques

  • 3D Rotation:
    x' = x·cosθ - y·sinθ
    y' = x·sinθ + y·cosθ
  • Perspective Projection:
    scale = d/(d + z)
  • Ray Tracing: Calculating light ray angles

Character Animation

Calculating knee bend for walking cycle:
θ(t) = 35° + 25°sin(2πt/1.2)
Where t is seconds in animation

GPU Optimization

Modern graphics cards implement:

  • Taylor series approximations for sin/cos
  • Lookup tables for common angles
  • Parallel computation of vertex transforms
